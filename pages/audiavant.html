New Project! Mustang-slaying grocery getter for my wife!

I'm jealous of my wife now. I bought her the avant, and I think I like it more than mine! Needs a little TLC but nothing I can't handle!
It has a substantial mod list that I wish I had on my sedan:
JHM Tune | JHM v1 Headers | JHM Nitrous | JHM LWCP | JHM Ported Intake Manifold | Apikol Rear Diff & Snub | Thor Skid Plate | JHM LW Rear Rotors | Fast Intentions Catback
